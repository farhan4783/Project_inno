1. PROJECT OVERVIEW & PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
This project is a premium, high-end digital showcase website built for 
Anas & Sons Jewellers, a family-run jewelry establishment founded in 1988 
located in Batla House, Okhla, New Delhi.

Unlike generic e-commerce templates that rely on self-serve checkout carts 
and generic buy buttons, this site is designed as an interactive "Digital 
Showroom." It recognizes that high-value jewelry purchasing (ranging from 
₹30,000 to ₹5,00,000+) in India is relationship-driven, trust-sensitive, 
and heavily dependent on daily gold market rates. 

The primary goal of the site is to let customers browse a highly curated, 
visually stunning catalog of gold, diamond, and silver masterpieces and 
seamlessly initiate a real conversation with the jeweler via pre-filled 
WhatsApp messages.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TECH STACK USED
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The website is engineered with a modern, fast, and optimized frontend stack:

- Framework: Next.js 16 (App Router configuration)
  Provides lightning-fast page loading times, pre-rendered static paths 
  for maximum SEO indexing, and optimized image processing.
  
- Language: TypeScript
  Enforces strict type definitions across product schemas, component props, 
  and business configs to prevent runtime errors.
  
- Styling: Tailwind CSS (v4 configuration)
  Used for responsive layouts, custom utility classes, animations, and the 
  curated color system (Noir & Gold themes).
  
- Animations: Framer Motion
  Powers all scroll-triggered reveal animations, mobile sidebar drawer 
  transitions, and interactive modal overlays with fluid bezier physics.
  
- Smooth Scroll: Lenis Scroll
  A light, high-performance smooth scroll library that standardizes scrolling 
  kinetics across Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android browsers.
  
- Icons: SVG Vectors
  All icons, markers, and buttons are built with lightweight inline SVGs to 
  reduce HTTP requests and maintain sharpness on retina displays.

3. DESIGN AESTHETICS & DESIGN SYSTEM
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The website features a rich, dark-mode, editorial aesthetic inspired by 
global luxury fashion houses:

- Color Palette:
  * Noir Base: #080808 (Main background, provides depth)
  * Noir Light: #0E0E0E (Section alternative background)
  * Noir Card: #111111 (Card background, stands out subtly)
  * Gold: #C6A55A (Primary accent color used for borders, headers, and highlights)
  * Gold Light: #E8D5A3 (Used for hover states and shimmering gradients)
  * Ivory: #F5F0E8 (Clean contrast color for header typography)
  * Champagne: #E8DCC8 (Softer off-white for description paragraphs)
  * Neutral Off-White: #F2F0EC (Used behind transparent product PNGs to 
    eliminate checkerboard patterns)

- Typography:
  * Serif font: Cormorant Garamond (Used for all luxury headings, italic 
    highlights, and quotes)
  * Sans-serif font: Inter (Used for numbers, specifications, labels, 
    and descriptive paragraphs)

- Key Visual Elements:
  * Gold Shimmer: Text gradients that catch the light dynamically.
  * Editorial Dividers: Subtle gold rules separating blocks.
  * Inset Collages: Overlapping secondary photos safely clipped inside 
    their parent grid blocks for an editorial magazine layout.
  * Scroll Indicator: A progress bar tracking scroll position at the top 
    of the viewport.
  * Custom Cursor: A luxury gold dot inside a soft gold ring that expands 
    and wraps interactive links on hover (safely disabled on touch screens).

4. KEY FEATURES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Interactive Product Catalog (/catalog)
  A standalone, searchable catalog housing 20 unique stock jewelry pieces.
  * Multi-criteria Filters: Category tabs, Metal/Material check-boxes, 
    Price Range check-boxes, and Occasion check-boxes.
  * Occasion Filter: Maps pieces into Bridal, Engagement, Daily Wear, 
    Gifting, and Festive buckets.
  * Real-Time Search: Filters by name, description, material, or category.
  * Sort Options: Sort by popularity, price (high-to-low/low-to-high), 
    and gross weight (heavy-to-light).

- Product Lightbox Details Modal
  Clicking any product card opens a screen-filling showcase overlay:
  * High-resolution zoom-view of the jewelry.
  * Direct product specs table: Product Reference (ID), Primary Metal, 
    Gold Purity, Gross Weight, and Target Gender.
  * WhatsApp CTA: Pre-fills a message with the product specs and price:
    "Hi Anas & Sons Jewellers, I am interested in viewing/purchasing... 
     Est. Price: ₹X,XX,XXX..."

- Immersive Showroom Video Section
  Integrates video loops showing Okhla's largest showroom space.

- Pinpoint Showroom Google Map
  Integrates a customized iframe pointing to the exact coordinates of the 
  showroom at Shop No. 334-G, Batla House. Styled with CSS filters to blend 
  into the website's dark aesthetic.

- AI Chat Assistant Component
  A floating conversation helper that suggests pre-defined user questions 
  (e.g., "Wedding jewellery suggestions", "Custom designs") and directs 
  inquiries straight to the sales team.

- WhatsApp FAB (Floating Action Button)
  A persistent button in the lower-right corner of the viewport allowing 
  instant chat access from any page.

5. PROJECT STRUCTURE & CODE ORGANIZATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The codebase is structured logically for easy maintenance and updates:

- `/public/products/`
  Houses the 20 categorized, high-resolution stock jewelry images renamed 
  systematically (e.g., `product-ring-01.jpg`, `product-necklace-02.jpg`).

- `/src/config/`
  * `site.ts`: The single source of truth for business contact details 
    (Phone: +919077000064, Address, Store Hours, Instagram link). Updating 
    details here changes them globally across all pages.
  * `products.ts`: The catalog database. Houses the specifications, price, 
    images, and descriptions of the 20 masterwork items.
  * `content.ts`: Editable text contents, collections copy, FAQs, 
    and testimonials.

- `/src/components/`
  * `Navbar.tsx` & `Footer.tsx`: Multi-page layout headers and footers.
  * `Hero.tsx`: Immersive video landing container.
  * `Collections.tsx`: Crafted for Every Occasion collection categories.
  * `FeaturedProducts.tsx`: Standardized horizontal slider for featured items.
  * `Heritage.tsx`: Timeline showing the family business legacy.
  * `Gallery.tsx`: Masonry Instagram feed that plays video overlays on hover.
  * `Testimonials.tsx`: Customer reviews block. (Configured to hide 
    automatically if the reviews array in content.ts is empty).
  * `FAQ.tsx`: Accordion list answering common customer concerns.
  * `VisitUs.tsx`: Store hours grid and Google Map.
  * `AIAssistant.tsx` & `WhatsAppFAB.tsx`: Floating chat utilities.

- `/src/app/`
  * `globals.css`: Contains CSS rules, variables, Lenis layout styles, 
    Tailwind base-layer modifiers, and hover cursor configurations.
  * `page.tsx`: Landing homepage flow combining sections separated by 
    custom gold lines.
  * `catalog/page.tsx`: Dedicated Next.js page routing for the Product Catalog.
